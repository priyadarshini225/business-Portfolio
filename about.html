About Me

Hello! I'm Priyadarshini, an artist who transforms emotions into art ✨

I specialize in pencil sketches, watercolor paintings, and charcoal portraits. From childhood, art has been my passion—a way to preserve stories, memories, and feelings.

With over 50+ commissioned artworks, I've created meaningful pieces for people who want to cherish special moments forever.

My mission is to make art accessible and personal. Whether you're looking for a thoughtful gift or something for yourself, I'm here to bring your ideas to life.

"Art is not just a visual—it’s a feeling captured on canvas."
